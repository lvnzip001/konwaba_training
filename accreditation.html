FAQ

Accreditation questions, answered clearly.

If you’re a learner, sponsor, employer or procurement team, these answers cover the usual verification topics.

KTI provides programmes across different training streams. Some are delivered under recognised accreditation or registration frameworks (where applicable). For formal verification, request the verification pack and specify the programme you are checking.

Employers can submit a verification request with learner details and programme name. We respond with the relevant evidence and verification information based on the programme stream and records available.

KTI is POPIA-aware and uses personal information only for learner administration, delivery, assessment and responding to enquiries. We do not sell personal information. Where appropriate, information access is limited to authorised staff.

We apply structured delivery planning, evidence management, moderation support where applicable, and controlled administration. These controls help ensure consistent outcomes and verifiable programme delivery.
